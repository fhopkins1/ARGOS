# EO-018 Seeker Department Framework

The Seeker Department is a deterministic collection of intelligence office
scaffolds. EO-018 does not perform market analysis, approve trades, execute
trades, or allow direct office-to-office communication.

## Offices

- Technical Analysis Office
- Fundamental Research Office
- Macroeconomic Office
- News & Sentiment Office
- Options Flow Office
- Cryptocurrency Office
- Event Intelligence Office
- Alternative Data Office

## Capabilities

- Office Registry
- Office Mailboxes
- Office Queues
- Office Instrument Panels
- Office Metrics
- Office Configuration
- Candidate Opportunity Report generation
- COR routing through Foundation Courier

COR payloads are scaffolded as unanalysed candidates. Later Seeker EOs may add
actual office-specific evidence production.
