# EO-DF Restart and Recovery Endurance

EO-DF supports restart endurance and recovery endurance campaign types.

Tracked requirements:
- no duplicate missions
- no duplicate workflows
- no duplicate tokens
- no duplicate fills
- no duplicate positions
- no duplicate truth
- no EO-CK duplication
- reconciliation completes or blocks

EO-DE owns injected failure campaigns. EO-DF schedules and measures bounded recovery campaigns.
