# EO-034 Derivatives Analysis Office

The Analyst-side Derivatives Analysis Office performs deterministic reasoning
over existing derivatives and options-flow intelligence. It evaluates volatility,
dealer positioning, gamma, delta, skew, open interest, expiration, and
institutional positioning without modifying Seeker reports.

## Analysts

- Volatility Analyst
- Options Flow Analyst
- Dealer Positioning Analyst
- Gamma Analyst
- Delta Analyst
- Skew Analyst
- Open Interest Analyst
- Expiration Analyst
- Institutional Positioning Analyst

## Capabilities

- Derivatives Analytical Assessment Reports (`AAR`)
- Derivatives Reasoning Graphs
- Competing Thesis Analysis (`DTA`)
- Probability Landscapes (`DPL`)
- Alternative Explanation Framework
- Office Instrument Panel

## Boundaries

The office does not recommend trades, execute trades, modify Seeker
intelligence, suppress competing explanations, or issue Command Decisions.
