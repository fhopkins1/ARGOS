# EO-060 Trader Operational Readiness Review

## Purpose

The Trader Operational Readiness Review Board (TORR) independently certifies whether the complete Trader Group is operationally ready before execution authority is granted. TORR introduces no new trading capability and does not determine trades, execute trades, or modify organizational policy.

## Certification Scope

- Trade Execution Office
- Order Management Office
- Broker Integration Office
- Execution Quality Office
- Position Management Office
- Trade Monitoring Office
- Trader Fusion Office
- Complete execution lifecycle from Executive approval through Historian recording
- Deterministic stress scenarios
- Audit, persistence, and traceability preservation

## Deterministic Stress Scenarios

TORR validates adverse execution conditions including broker failure, network failure, exchange outage, partial fills, order rejection, duplicate execution attempts, position reconciliation failures, high market volatility, system restart, market halt, excessive latency, and infrastructure failure.

Every scenario must preserve execution integrity, auditability, deterministic behavior, and operational records.

## Certification Outcomes

- `CERTIFIED`
- `CERTIFIED WITH OBSERVATIONS`
- `CONDITIONALLY CERTIFIED`
- `NOT CERTIFIED`

Certification is granted only when deterministic tests, readiness checks, stress tests, audit reconstruction, and evidence preservation pass.

## Produced Artifacts

- Trader Operational Readiness Report
- Trader Certification Report
- Trader Integration Validation Report
- Determinism Verification Report
- Traceability Verification Report
- Execution Stress Test Results
- Enterprise Readiness Assessment
- Trader Readiness Case File
- Trader Certification Archive
- Corrective Action Register

## Governance Boundaries

TORR may certify, block, document deficiencies, require corrective action, and preserve evidence. It may not authorize trades, alter Executive decisions, overwrite readiness evidence, discard test results, or grant live execution authority outside explicit approved gates.
