# EO-DF Staged Duration Model

EO-DF supports:
- Stage 0 Shakedown
- Stage 1 Accelerated 24-hour event-time campaign
- Stage 2 One-hour wall-clock campaign
- Stage 3 Eight-hour wall-clock campaign
- Stage 4 Twenty-four-hour wall-clock campaign
- Stage 5 Seventy-two-hour wall-clock campaign
- Stage 6 Extended multi-day campaign

Accelerated event time and wall-clock duration are stored separately. Segmented campaigns record cumulative duration and longest uninterrupted duration independently.
