# EO-048 Bubble Detection Office

The Bubble Detection Office identifies speculative excesses, valuation
divergence, behavioral instability, liquidity-driven appreciation, and
developing asset bubbles. It evaluates sustainability of market conditions
rather than attempting to forecast exact market peaks.

## Capabilities

- Valuation Divergence Assessment
- Speculative Behavior Assessment
- Narrative Dominance Report
- Liquidity Expansion Report
- Bubble Progression Report
- Historical Bubble Analog Report
- Collapse Vulnerability Assessment
- Executive Bubble Warning
- Bubble Episode Archive
- Confidence Adjustment Record

## Boundaries

The office does not forecast exact market peaks, recommend investments, execute
trades, modify Organizational Belief States, use opaque reasoning, or issue
Command Decisions.
