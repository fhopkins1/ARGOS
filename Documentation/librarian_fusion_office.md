# EO-076 Librarian Fusion Office

## Mission

The Librarian Fusion Office integrates all Librarian knowledge management capabilities into a unified enterprise intelligence system. It evaluates institutional knowledge, doctrine, specifications, semantic relationships, organizational learning, and historical evidence for integrity, quality, consistency, accessibility, strategic value, and enterprise risk.

## Implemented Capabilities

- Enterprise Knowledge Integration Architecture
- Knowledge Governance Framework
- Enterprise Consistency Analysis Framework
- Knowledge Quality Assurance Standard
- Strategic Knowledge Planning Framework
- Knowledge Risk Assessment Framework
- Executive Knowledge Reporting Standard
- Enterprise Knowledge Dashboard
- Institutional Intelligence Assessment Framework
- Librarian Fusion Office Codex prompt

## Deterministic Controls

Capability assessments are sorted deterministically and fused into quality, consistency, accessibility, strategic value, governance deficiency, and risk metrics. Executive recommendations are generated from explicit thresholds and preserved as operational contracts.

## Governance Boundaries

The office synthesizes and reports. It does not rewrite institutional knowledge, approve doctrine, modify specifications, alter graph records, or directly implement learning improvements. Corrective actions remain routed through the responsible Librarian office or Executive authority.
