# EO-039 Analyst Operational Readiness Review

The Analyst Operational Readiness Review verifies that the Analyst Department
operates as a deterministic reasoning organization. It executes integration
checks across all Analyst offices, validates analytical artifacts, verifies
replay and audit reconstruction, and produces readiness authorization for the
Risk Office.

## Verified Offices

- Statistical Analysis Office
- Technical Analysis Office
- Fundamental Analysis Office
- Macroeconomic Analysis Office
- Derivatives Analysis Office
- Behavioral Analysis Office
- Risk Interaction Office
- Cross-Discipline Review Office
- Analytical Fusion Office

## Verified Artifacts

- Decision Models
- Reasoning Graphs
- Competing Thesis Analyses
- Probability Landscapes
- Unified Decision Models
- Organizational Reasoning Graphs
- Organizational Belief States
- Independent Evidence Scores
- Intellectual Diversity Scores

## Readiness Outputs

- Analyst Operational Readiness Review (`A-ORR`)
- Analyst Completion Report (`ACR-001`)
- Authorization to Proceed to Group 5 - Risk Office

Certification is granted only when readiness checks, replay, audit
reconstruction, and deterministic tests pass.
