# EO-DC Commander and Operator Model

`TruthPromotionAuthority.commander_read_model()` exposes read-only promotion status.

Commander may view approvals, rejections, degraded records, quarantined records, revoked approvals, domain violations, and learning eligibility.

Commander cannot manually convert proof to paper, mark unverified evidence verified, override critical promotion bypasses, rewrite promotion history, erase rejection evidence, or enable live promotion.
