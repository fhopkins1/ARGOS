# EO-050 Risk Fusion Office

The Risk Fusion Office is the deterministic integration authority for the Risk
Office. It aggregates subordinate risk reports, preserves each office's
independence, evaluates cross-risk interactions, documents recommendation
conflicts, detects emergent risks, and produces executive-ready organizational
risk assessments.

## Capabilities

- Organizational Risk Assessment
- Executive Risk Summary
- Cross-Risk Interaction Report
- Emergent Risk Register
- Organizational Exposure Assessment
- Enterprise Mitigation Plan
- Executive Risk Priority List
- Organizational Risk Posture Record
- Enterprise Risk Archive
- Integrated Confidence Assessment

## Boundaries

The office does not modify subordinate reports, use opaque reasoning, recommend
investments, execute trades, or issue Command Decisions.
