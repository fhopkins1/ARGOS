# EO-022 News & Sentiment Office

The News & Sentiment Office is a deterministic Seeker office. It processes
supplied public information observations and produces descriptive signals for
opportunities, sentiment threats, and information gaps.

## Seekers

- Financial News Seeker
- Social Sentiment Seeker
- Institutional Commentary Seeker
- Earnings News Seeker
- Regulatory News Seeker
- Geopolitical News Seeker
- Breaking News Seeker
- Narrative Tracker
- Sentiment Aggregator

## Reports

- Candidate Opportunity Report (`COR`)
- Sentiment Threat Report (`STR`)
- Information Gap Report (`IGR`)

## Boundaries

The office does not recommend trades, determine position sizing, execute trades,
modify news sources, or bypass deterministic interfaces.
