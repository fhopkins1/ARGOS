# EO-021 Macroeconomic Office

The Macroeconomic Office is a deterministic Seeker office. It processes supplied
macroeconomic observations and produces descriptive signals for opportunities,
threats, and information gaps.

## Seekers

- Inflation Seeker
- Interest Rate Seeker
- Labor Market Seeker
- GDP Seeker
- Monetary Policy Seeker
- Fiscal Policy Seeker
- Currency Seeker
- Bond Market Seeker
- Commodity Seeker
- Global Economy Seeker

## Reports

- Candidate Opportunity Report (`COR`)
- Macroeconomic Threat Report (`MTR`)
- Information Gap Report (`IGR`)

## Boundaries

The office does not recommend trades, determine position sizes, execute trades,
perform company valuation, or bypass deterministic interfaces.
