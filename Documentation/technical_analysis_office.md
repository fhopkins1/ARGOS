# EO-019 Technical Analysis Office

The Technical Analysis Office is a deterministic Seeker office. It processes
provided market observations and produces technical signals for Candidate
Opportunity Reports. It does not fetch data, discover opportunities, recommend
trades, determine sizing, or execute trades.

## Seekers

- Trend Seeker
- Momentum Seeker
- Volatility Seeker
- Market Structure Seeker
- Pattern Recognition Seeker
- Volume Seeker
- Technical Screener

## Outputs

The office generates CORs routed through Foundation Courier. Technical signals
are descriptive inputs only and carry no trade recommendation.
