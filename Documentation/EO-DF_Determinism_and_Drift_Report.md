# EO-DF Determinism and Drift Report

EO-DF computes a determinism signature from stable sample shapes and findings. Wall-clock timestamps are excluded.

Drift summaries include minimum, maximum, mean, median, slope, baseline delta, and drift flag for memory, task count, queues, messages, cache, journal size, checkpoint size, latency, and cost.

Unexplained financial-state nondeterminism remains a critical failure for later Series D orders.
