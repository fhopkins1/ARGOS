# EO-046 Tail Risk Office

The Tail Risk Office identifies, models, evaluates, and mitigates
low-probability, high-impact risks capable of materially threatening ARGOS. No
catastrophic scenario is discarded solely because its estimated probability is
low.

## Capabilities

- Deterministic catastrophic scenario generation
- Distribution tail evaluation
- Maximum Credible Loss calculation
- Dependency Cascade Analysis
- Nonlinear Exposure Assessment
- Historical Analog comparison
- Organizational Tail Exposure Summary
- Mitigation Recommendation Records
- Tail Scenario Archive
- Confidence Adjustment Records

## Produced Artifacts

- Tail Risk Assessment Report
- Extreme Scenario Report
- Maximum Credible Loss Record
- Dependency Cascade Analysis
- Nonlinear Exposure Assessment
- Historical Analog Report
- Organizational Tail Exposure Summary
- Mitigation Recommendation Record
- Tail Scenario Archive
- Confidence Adjustment Record

## Boundaries

The office does not use opaque reasoning, discard low-probability catastrophic
scenarios, recommend investments, execute trades, modify Organizational Belief
States, or issue Command Decisions.
