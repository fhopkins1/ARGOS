# EO-040 Risk Office Framework

The Risk Office Framework creates ARGOS's independent organizational safeguard
layer. Risk offices consume only Organizational Belief States from the certified
Analyst Department and generate Risk Assessment Reports without market
discovery, trade recommendations, execution authority, or modification of
Analyst belief states.

## Offices

- Position Risk Office
- Portfolio Risk Office
- Liquidity Risk Office
- Correlation Risk Office
- Volatility Risk Office
- Tail Risk Office
- Black Swan Office
- Bubble Detection Office
- Recovery Planning Office
- Risk Fusion Office

## Capabilities

- Risk Assessment Reports (`RAR`)
- Risk Office Registry
- Risk Office Queues
- Risk Office Instrument Panels
- Courier routing
- Audit-backed document creation and transfer
- Persistence-backed report storage
- Prompt Snapshot integration

## Boundaries

Risk offices do not recommend trades, execute trades, modify Organizational
Belief States, perform market discovery, or override deterministic interfaces.
