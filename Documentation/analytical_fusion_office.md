# EO-038 Analytical Fusion Office

The Analyst-side Analytical Fusion Office deterministically integrates completed
Analyst work into a Unified Decision Model. It preserves source traceability,
disagreement, uncertainty, probability landscapes, reasoning graphs, and
historian liaison metadata.

## Engines

- Decision Model Integrator
- Reasoning Graph Integrator
- Probability Landscape Integrator
- Consensus Engine
- Conflict Engine
- Organizational Reasoning Engine
- Analyst Confidence Engine
- Fusion Historian Liaison

## Capabilities

- Unified Decision Model (`UDM`)
- Organizational Reasoning Graph (`ORG`)
- Analytical Fusion Report (`AFR`)
- Independent Evidence Score
- Intellectual Diversity Score
- Organizational Confidence
- Office Instrument Panel

## Boundaries

The office does not create new analyses, modify analyst conclusions, suppress
disagreement, discard uncertainty, or issue Command Decisions.
