# EO-030 Statistical Analysis Office

The Statistical Analysis Office is the Analyst Department office responsible for
deterministic quantitative reasoning over existing evidence.

## Analysts

- Probability Analyst
- Distribution Analyst
- Correlation Analyst
- Regression Analyst
- Bayesian Analyst
- Time Series Analyst
- Monte Carlo Analyst
- Confidence Analyst

## Capabilities

- Statistical Analytical Assessment Reports (`AAR`)
- Mandatory Argument Maps
- Probability estimation
- Confidence interval estimation
- Expected value calculation
- Sensitivity analysis
- Model comparison

## Boundaries

The office does not recommend trades, execute trades, modify Seeker reports,
suppress contradictory evidence, issue Command Decisions, or override Risk
Office.
