# EO-027 Multi-Office Intelligence Fusion Office

The Fusion Office is a deterministic Seeker synthesis office. It consumes
existing Seeker reports, normalizes their preserved signals, detects agreement
and conflict, classifies evidence, prioritizes findings, and generates
multi-office synthesis reports.

## Engines

- Corroboration Engine
- Conflict Detection Engine
- Confidence Aggregator
- Evidence Classification Engine
- Intelligence Prioritizer
- MIR Generator

## Reports

- Multi-Office Intelligence Report (`MIR`)
- Conflict Report (`CFR`)
- Intelligence Corroboration Report (`ICR`)

## Boundaries

The office does not create original intelligence, modify Seeker reports,
recommend trades, average confidence scores, or ignore conflicting evidence.
