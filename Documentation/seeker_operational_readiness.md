# EO-028 Seeker Operational Readiness Review

The Seeker Operational Readiness Review verifies the Seeker Department as a
deterministic intelligence organization before Analyst Group implementation.

## Verified Offices

- Technical Analysis Office
- Fundamental Research Office
- Macroeconomic Office
- News & Sentiment Office
- Options Flow Office
- Cryptocurrency Office
- Event Intelligence Office
- Alternative Data Office
- Multi-Office Intelligence Fusion Office

## Verified Capabilities

- Candidate Opportunity Reports
- Threat Reports
- Information Gap Reports
- Multi-Office Intelligence Reports
- Independent Evidence Scores
- Complete Seeker intelligence replay
- Courier, audit, persistence, configuration, and prompt repository integration

## Readiness Artifacts

- Seeker Operational Readiness Review (`S-ORR`)
- Seeker Completion Report (`SCR-001`)
- Authorization to Proceed to Group 4 - Analyst Group

## Boundaries

Failed readiness tests, failed replay, failed audit reconstruction, or failed
office integration block Seeker certification and Analyst Group authorization.
