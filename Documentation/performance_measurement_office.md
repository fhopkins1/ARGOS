# EO-062 Performance Measurement Office

## Purpose

The Performance Measurement Office is ARGOS's deterministic organizational performance authority. It objectively measures every major organizational function through reproducible empirical metrics without influencing active operations.

## Measurement Scope

- Executive Group performance
- Seeker and intelligence performance
- Analyst performance
- Risk Office performance
- Trader Group performance
- Enterprise-wide organizational performance
- Long-term performance trends
- Deterministic organizational rankings

## Produced Artifacts

- `ORGANIZATIONAL_PERFORMANCE_REPORT`
- `EXECUTIVE_PERFORMANCE_REPORT`
- `ORGANIZATIONAL_SCORECARD`
- `PERFORMANCE_TREND_REPORT`
- Organizational Metrics Registry
- Group performance datasets
- Metric calculation traces
- Historical comparison records
- Deterministic performance archive
- Performance measurement standards

## Deterministic Metrics

The office calculates standardized metrics including decision accuracy, risk prediction accuracy, execution quality, evidence quality, prompt effectiveness, organizational consistency, model performance, process reliability, learning velocity, and composite ranking scores.

Every metric is derived from preserved empirical datasets and traceable audit/source case identifiers.

## Historical Analysis

Performance scorecards are archived immutably. Historical comparisons classify long-term changes as improving, stable, or declining using deterministic thresholds.

## Governance Boundaries

The Performance Measurement Office measures and reports only. It does not gather market intelligence, make decisions, execute trades, influence active operations, overwrite historical performance, or directly modify organizational behavior.

## Librarian Deliverables

- Performance Measurement Standards
- Organizational Metric Definitions
- Historical Performance Methodology
