# EO-035 Behavioral Analysis Office

The Analyst-side Behavioral Analysis Office performs deterministic reasoning
over existing sentiment, narrative, crowd, institutional, retail, and
reflexivity intelligence. Every analysis produces a Decision Model, preserves
competing behavioral hypotheses, and generates a Probability Landscape.

## Analysts

- Crowd Psychology Analyst
- Narrative Analyst
- Sentiment Analyst
- Institutional Behavior Analyst
- Retail Behavior Analyst
- Fear and Greed Analyst
- Market Regime Psychologist
- Behavioral Bias Analyst
- Reflexivity Analyst

## Capabilities

- Behavioral Analytical Assessment Reports (`AAR`)
- Decision Models (`BDM`)
- Behavioral Reasoning Graphs
- Competing Thesis Analysis (`BTA`)
- Probability Landscapes (`BPL`)
- Alternative Explanation Framework
- Office Instrument Panel

## Boundaries

The office does not recommend trades, execute trades, modify Seeker
intelligence, suppress competing explanations, or issue Command Decisions.
