# EO-033 Macroeconomic Analysis Office

The Analyst-side Macroeconomic Analysis Office performs deterministic reasoning
over existing macroeconomic intelligence. It classifies regimes, evaluates
competing theses, and preserves alternative explanations.

## Analysts

- Inflation Analyst
- Monetary Policy Analyst
- Fiscal Policy Analyst
- Labor Market Analyst
- Business Cycle Analyst
- Yield Curve Analyst
- Currency Analyst
- Commodity Analyst
- Global Economy Analyst

## Capabilities

- Macroeconomic Analytical Assessment Reports (`AAR`)
- Macroeconomic Reasoning Graphs
- Competing Thesis Analysis (`CTA`)
- Economic Regime Classification
- Alternative Explanation Framework

## Boundaries

The office does not recommend trades, execute trades, modify Seeker
intelligence, suppress competing hypotheses, or issue Command Decisions.
