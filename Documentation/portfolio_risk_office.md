# EO-042 Portfolio Risk Office

The Portfolio Risk Office evaluates portfolio-level risk across multiple
positions. It integrates Position Risk Office outputs and Analyst-derived
Organizational Belief States without recommending investments, executing
trades, modifying belief states, or issuing Command Decisions.

## Organization

- Office Chief
- Correlation Analyst
- Concentration Analyst
- Diversification Analyst
- Sector Exposure Analyst
- Geographic Exposure Analyst
- Macroeconomic Exposure Analyst
- Systemic Risk Analyst
- Portfolio Stress Analyst
- Failure Cascade Analyst

## Capabilities

- Portfolio Risk Model
- Portfolio Stress Testing
- Historical Adversary Library (`HAL`)
- Correlation Matrix generation
- Portfolio Readiness Score
- Organizational Confidence Surface evaluation
- Office Instrument Panel

## Boundaries

The office does not recommend investments, execute trades, modify
Organizational Belief States, issue Command Decisions, or override deterministic
interfaces.
