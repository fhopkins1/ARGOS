# EO-026 Alternative Data Office

The Alternative Data Office is a deterministic Seeker office. It processes
supplied real-world indicator observations and produces descriptive signals for
opportunities, threats, information gaps, and alternative intelligence.

## Seekers

- Satellite Imagery Seeker
- Shipping & Logistics Seeker
- Web Traffic Seeker
- Consumer Activity Seeker
- Energy Consumption Seeker
- Weather Intelligence Seeker
- Supply Chain Intelligence Seeker
- Patent Activity Seeker
- Employment Intelligence Seeker

## Reports

- Candidate Opportunity Report (`COR`)
- Alternative Data Threat Report (`ATR`)
- Information Gap Report (`IGR`)
- Alternative Intelligence Report (`AIR`)

## Boundaries

The office does not recommend trades, determine position sizing, execute trades,
bypass deterministic interfaces, or ignore Risk Office doctrine.
