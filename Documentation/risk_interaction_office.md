# EO-036 Risk Interaction Office

The Analyst-side Risk Interaction Office deterministically pressure-tests
Decision Models produced by Analyst offices. It identifies weak assumptions,
generates stress scenarios, documents contradictions, records uncertainty and
missing evidence, and recommends confidence adjustments without modifying the
reviewed model.

## Analysts

- Assumption Auditor
- Scenario Analyst
- Stress Test Analyst
- Contradiction Analyst
- Uncertainty Analyst
- Missing Evidence Analyst
- Failure Mode Analyst
- Devil's Advocate Analyst

## Capabilities

- Risk Readiness Reports (`RRR`)
- Analytical Stress Tests (`AST`)
- Decision Model Challenge Reports (`DMCR`)
- Risk Readiness Scoring
- Confidence Adjustment Recommendations
- Risk Interaction Reasoning Graphs
- Office Instrument Panel

## Boundaries

The office does not recommend trades, override the Risk Office, modify Decision
Models, execute trades, or issue Command Decisions.
