# EO-043 Liquidity Risk Office

The Liquidity Risk Office deterministically evaluates execution feasibility and
liquidity risk. It estimates slippage, market impact, spread cost, market depth,
order book coverage, exit feasibility, and liquidity stress without executing
trades or recommending investments.

## Organization

- Office Chief
- Market Depth Analyst
- Slippage Analyst
- Execution Analyst
- Spread Analyst
- Order Book Analyst
- Exit Strategy Analyst
- Liquidity Stress Analyst
- Market Impact Analyst

## Capabilities

- Liquidity Risk Model
- Execution Feasibility Report
- Liquidity Stress Report
- Liquidity Readiness Score
- Historical liquidity stress simulations
- Office Instrument Panel

## Boundaries

The office does not recommend investments, execute trades, modify
Organizational Belief States, issue Command Decisions, or ignore historical
liquidity events.
