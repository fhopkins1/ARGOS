# EO-032 Fundamental Analysis Office

The Analyst-side Fundamental Analysis Office performs deterministic business
analysis over existing fundamental intelligence. It is distinct from the Seeker
Fundamental Research Office and does not discover opportunities.

## Analysts

- Financial Statement Analyst
- Valuation Analyst
- Competitive Advantage Analyst
- Capital Allocation Analyst
- Management Quality Analyst
- Earnings Quality Analyst
- Balance Sheet Analyst
- Cash Flow Analyst
- Industry Structure Analyst

## Capabilities

- Fundamental Analytical Assessment Reports (`AAR`)
- Fundamental Reasoning Graphs
- Business Quality scoring
- Economic Moat evaluation
- Alternative business explanation generation

## Boundaries

The office does not recommend trades, execute trades, modify Seeker
intelligence, ignore contradictory evidence, or issue Command Decisions.
