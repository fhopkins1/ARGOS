# EO-024 Cryptocurrency Office

The Cryptocurrency Office is a deterministic Seeker office. It processes
supplied blockchain ecosystem observations and produces descriptive signals for
opportunities, threats, information gaps, and blockchain activity.

## Seekers

- Price Structure Seeker
- Blockchain Activity Seeker
- Stablecoin Flow Seeker
- Exchange Flow Seeker
- Wallet Activity Seeker
- Institutional Crypto Seeker
- DeFi Seeker
- NFT Market Seeker
- On-Chain Analytics Seeker

## Reports

- Candidate Opportunity Report (`COR`)
- Cryptocurrency Threat Report (`CTR`)
- Information Gap Report (`IGR`)
- Blockchain Activity Report (`CXR`)

## Boundaries

The office does not recommend trades, determine position sizing, execute trades,
bypass deterministic interfaces, or ignore Risk Office doctrine.
