# EO-029 Analyst Department Framework

The Analyst Department is ARGOS's deterministic reasoning organization. It
consumes existing Seeker and Fusion reports, generates hypotheses, evaluates
evidence, records alternative explanations, and produces Analytical Assessment
Reports.

## Offices

- Statistical Analysis Office
- Technical Analysis Office
- Fundamental Analysis Office
- Macroeconomic Analysis Office
- Derivatives Analysis Office
- Behavioral Analysis Office
- Risk Interaction Office
- Cross-Discipline Review Office
- Analytical Fusion Office

## Frameworks

- Hypothesis Framework
- Evidence Evaluation Framework
- Alternative Explanation Framework
- Office Instrument Panels
- Analytical Assessment Report (`AAR`) generation

## Boundaries

The Analyst Department does not discover opportunities, recommend trades,
execute trades, override Risk Office, or modify Seeker reports.
