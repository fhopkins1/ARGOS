# EO-082 Case Study Office

## Mission

The Case Study Office creates immersive educational experiences from historical financial events, investment decisions, market environments, and organizational learning.

## Implemented Capabilities

- Case Study Architecture
- Historical Reconstruction Framework
- Decision Simulation Framework
- Multi-Perspective Analysis Standard
- Alternative History Framework
- Personalized Case Selection Engine
- Case Validation Standard
- Educational Analytics Framework
- Case Study Dashboard
- Case Study System Prompt

## Deterministic Historical Reconstruction

Case studies reconstruct authentic decision environments using only information available at the decision timestamp. Outcome-revealing or later evidence is excluded from the pre-outcome simulation and retained only as excluded evidence metadata.

## Educational Simulation

Students must evaluate evidence, assess risk, identify alternatives, and justify reasoning before historical outcomes are revealed. Multiple perspectives preserve balanced analysis and prevent one-dimensional hindsight.

## Boundary

The Case Study Office teaches from validated history. It does not create new market truth, provide investment advice, or reveal historical outcomes before the learner submits reasoning.
