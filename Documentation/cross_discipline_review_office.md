# EO-037 Cross-Discipline Review Office

The Analyst-side Cross-Discipline Review Office performs deterministic peer
review across Analyst disciplines. It compares evidence, reasoning, unknowns,
assumptions, confidence, and conclusions while preserving disagreement.

## Reviewers

- Statistical Reviewer
- Technical Reviewer
- Fundamental Reviewer
- Macroeconomic Reviewer
- Derivatives Reviewer
- Behavioral Reviewer
- Risk Interaction Reviewer
- Consensus Analyst

## Capabilities

- Cross-Discipline Review Reports (`CDRR`)
- Consensus Reports (`CR`)
- Disagreement Reports (`DR`)
- Analytical Conflict Reports (`ACR`)
- Agreement Scoring
- Conflict Scoring
- Evidence Comparison
- Reasoning Comparison
- Unknown Comparison
- Office Instrument Panel

## Boundaries

The office does not recommend trades, modify analyses, suppress disagreement,
force consensus, or issue Command Decisions.
