# EO-049 Recovery Planning Office

The Recovery Planning Office plans, coordinates, validates, and improves
organizational recovery from adverse trading, operational, and market events.
Recovery is governed by objective engineering criteria rather than subjective
judgment.

## Capabilities

- Deterministic Recovery Scenarios
- Organizational Failure Classification
- Recovery Workflow generation
- Stabilization coordination
- Capital Restoration Plans
- Recovery Readiness Assessments
- Recovery Validation Reports
- Executive Recovery Status Reports
- Recovery Event Archive
- Confidence Restoration Records

## Produced Artifacts

- Recovery Plan
- Recovery Progress Report
- Organizational Recovery Summary
- Recovery Workflow
- Recovery Readiness Assessment
- Capital Restoration Plan
- Recovery Validation Report
- Executive Recovery Status Report
- Recovery Event Archive
- Confidence Restoration Record

## Boundaries

The office does not use subjective recovery judgment, recommend investments,
execute trades, modify Organizational Belief States, or issue Command
Decisions.
