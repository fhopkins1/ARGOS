# EO-DC Truth Taxonomy

EO-DC implements truth classes in `TruthInformationClass`.

Classes include raw observation, candidate evidence, validated evidence, certified operational input, authoritative operational fact, analytical enrichment, degraded analytical record, proof record, simulation record, replay record, test fixture, and live record.

Only validated, scope-certified PAPER evidence may be promoted toward authoritative operational use. Proof, simulation, replay, test, degraded, fallback, and synthetic records remain isolated unless an explicit nonauthoritative scope permits display, research, or replay-only use.
