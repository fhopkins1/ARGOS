# EO-080 Curriculum Office

## Mission

The Curriculum Office designs, maintains, and continuously optimizes personalized educational pathways that transform validated ARGOS institutional intelligence into measurable financial competence.

## Implemented Capabilities

- Curriculum Architecture
- Curriculum Map
- Deterministic Prerequisite Relationships
- Personalized Learning Pathway
- Curriculum Traceability Record
- Curriculum Adaptation Record
- Curriculum Effectiveness Metrics
- Curriculum Dashboard
- Curriculum Version History
- Curriculum Office System Prompt

## Deterministic Curriculum

Curricula are competency driven and ordered by explicit prerequisite relationships. Students progress according to demonstrated understanding, knowledge gaps, educational goals, learning velocity, investment interests, preferred learning style, and assessment results.

## Traceability

Every curriculum module links lessons, historical case studies, guided practice, assessments, advanced topics, reinforcement, validated knowledge, doctrine, specifications, and supporting evidence. Previous curriculum versions remain preserved and reconstructable.

## Boundary

The Curriculum Office organizes validated knowledge into learning pathways. It does not create new institutional knowledge, determine financial truth, or evaluate student competency.
