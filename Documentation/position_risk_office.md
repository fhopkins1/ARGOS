# Position Risk Office

The Position Risk Office evaluates the risk of a single proposed position in
isolation. It deliberately ignores portfolio interactions and assumes every
position is dangerous until demonstrated otherwise.

## Organization

- Office Chief
- Exposure Analyst
- Stop Loss Analyst
- Scenario Analyst
- Failure Mode Analyst
- Liquidity Analyst
- Position Sizing Analyst
- Sensitivity Analyst
- Invalidity Analyst
- Incoming Mailbox
- Outgoing Mailbox
- Office Instrument Panel
- Office Registry

## Position Risk Model

Every reviewed position receives:

- Maximum Expected Loss
- Expected Drawdown
- Probability of Failure
- Probability of Success
- Invalidation Conditions
- Position Sensitivity
- Recovery Potential
- Recommended Position Limit
- Confidence
- Organizational Confidence Surface Contribution

## Stress Tests

Every position is evaluated under Market Crash, Gap Down, Gap Up, Volatility
Spike, Liquidity Collapse, Interest Rate Shock, Regulatory Event, Unexpected
Earnings, Geopolitical Event, Execution Failure, and Historical Adversaries.

## Report

Position Risk Reports are Risk Assessment Reports (`RAR`) containing risk ID,
case file ID, trade cycle ID, asset, position size, maximum loss, expected loss,
probability distribution, failure modes, stress results, historical analogues,
invalidation criteria, confidence, Commander questions, and timestamp.

## Boundaries

The office does not recommend investments, modify analyses, evaluate portfolio
interactions, execute trades, issue Command Decisions, or override Executive
authority.
