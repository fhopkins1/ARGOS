# EO-051 Risk Operational Readiness Review

The Risk Operational Readiness Review certifies the Risk Office as a complete,
deterministic organizational subsystem suitable for operational deployment. It
introduces no additional analytical capability.

## Certification Scope

- Implementation completeness
- Interface validation
- Deterministic execution
- Organizational traceability
- Integrated workflow validation
- Adversarial testing
- Organizational consistency
- Executive readiness
- Trader Group prerequisite handoff
- Certification archival

## Produced Artifacts

- Risk Operational Readiness Report
- Risk Certification Report
- Risk Integration Validation Report
- Determinism Verification Report
- Traceability Verification Report
- Adversarial Test Results
- Organizational Consistency Assessment
- Executive Certification Record
- Risk Office Certification Archive
- Corrective Action Register

## Certification Rule

Risk Office certification is granted only when readiness checks, deterministic
tests, traceability, adversarial validation, Executive consumption, Trader
handoff, and certification archival all pass.
