# EO-DH Empty, Zero, None, and Default Semantics

EO-DH introduces `UnknownStateRecord` to distinguish unavailable, unknown, stale, pending, unresolved, blocked, rejected, degraded, not applicable, known zero, and known empty.

Unknown price, risk, position quantity, P&L, result, fill list, or office output must not be coerced into zero, empty, success, approved, filled, closed, or complete.
