# EO-031 Technical Analysis Office

The Analyst-side Technical Analysis Office performs deterministic reasoning over
existing technical intelligence. It is distinct from the Seeker Technical
Analysis Office and does not discover opportunities.

## Analysts

- Trend Analyst
- Momentum Analyst
- Market Structure Analyst
- Volume Analyst
- Pattern Reliability Analyst
- Volatility Analyst
- Relative Strength Analyst
- Multi-Timeframe Analyst
- Technical Fusion Analyst

## Capabilities

- Technical Analytical Assessment Reports (`AAR`)
- Technical Argument Graphs
- Historical Pattern Reliability evaluation
- Alternative Technical Explanation generation

## Boundaries

The office does not recommend trades, discover opportunities, execute trades,
modify Seeker intelligence, ignore contradictory evidence, issue Command
Decisions, or override Risk Office.
