# 5. Risk Office

Risk controls, exposure limits, safety boundaries, override mechanisms, and independent review surfaces.

## EO-040 Risk Office Framework

Risk Office Framework creates deterministic safeguard offices, consumes only Organizational Belief States, generates Risk Assessment Reports, routes through Courier, persists reports, and exposes Risk Office Instrument Panels.

## Position Risk Office

Position Risk Office evaluates one proposed position in isolation, generates Position Risk Models, stress tests, Position Readiness Scores, and Position Risk Reports without evaluating portfolio interactions or recommending investments.

## EO-042 Portfolio Risk Office

Portfolio Risk Office evaluates portfolio-level risk, generates correlation matrices, Historical Adversary simulations, failure cascade models, Portfolio Risk Models, Portfolio Readiness Scores, and Organizational Confidence Surface evaluations.

## EO-043 Liquidity Risk Office

Liquidity Risk Office evaluates execution feasibility, slippage, market impact, order book depth, liquidity stress, historical liquidity events, and Liquidity Readiness Scores without executing trades.

## EO-045 Volatility Risk Office

Volatility Risk Office measures realized and implied volatility, classifies regimes, forecasts volatility, detects shocks and contagion, updates the Organizational Confidence Surface, archives volatility events, and produces risk-only position adjustment recommendations.

## EO-046 Tail Risk Office

Tail Risk Office models catastrophic scenarios, evaluates distribution tails, calculates Maximum Credible Loss, analyzes dependency cascades and nonlinear exposures, compares historical analogs, archives all modeled scenarios, and updates the Organizational Confidence Surface.

## EO-048 Bubble Detection Office

Bubble Detection Office evaluates valuation divergence, speculative behavior, narrative dominance, liquidity expansion, bubble progression, historical analogs, collapse vulnerability, and defensive organizational warnings without forecasting exact market peaks.

## EO-049 Recovery Planning Office

Recovery Planning Office generates deterministic recovery plans, workflows, stabilization activities, capital restoration plans, readiness assessments, validation reports, procedural improvements, and recovery event archives.

## EO-050 Risk Fusion Office

Risk Fusion Office integrates subordinate Risk Office reports into an Organizational Risk Assessment, Executive Risk Summary, Cross-Risk Interaction Report, Emergent Risk Register, Enterprise Mitigation Plan, and Integrated Confidence Assessment.

## EO-051 Risk Operational Readiness Review

Risk Operational Readiness Review certifies implementation completeness, deterministic behavior, traceability, adversarial testing, organizational consistency, Executive readiness, Trader handoff, and certification archival.
