# 3. Seeker Group

Information discovery, source intake, search workflows, and initial collection processes.

## EO-018 Seeker Department Framework

Seeker Department offices, registry, instrumentation, COR generation, and Courier routing live in `src/argos/seeker`.

## EO-019 Technical Analysis Office

Technical Analysis Office processes supplied observations into deterministic technical signals and COR scaffolds without trade recommendations.

## EO-020 Fundamental Research Office

Fundamental Research Office processes supplied company fundamentals into deterministic fundamental signals and COR scaffolds without trade recommendations.

## EO-021 Macroeconomic Office

Macroeconomic Office processes supplied economic observations into deterministic opportunity, threat, and information gap reports without trade recommendations.

## EO-022 News & Sentiment Office

News & Sentiment Office processes supplied public information observations into deterministic opportunity, sentiment threat, and information gap reports without modifying sources.

## EO-023 Options Flow Office

Options Flow Office processes supplied derivatives observations into deterministic opportunity, institutional activity, threat, and information gap reports without trade authority.

## EO-024 Cryptocurrency Office

Cryptocurrency Office processes supplied blockchain ecosystem observations into deterministic opportunity, threat, information gap, and blockchain activity reports without trade authority.

## EO-025 Event Intelligence Office

Event Intelligence Office processes supplied objective market-event observations into deterministic opportunity, threat, information gap, and event assessment reports without trade authority.

## EO-026 Alternative Data Office

Alternative Data Office processes supplied real-world indicator observations into deterministic opportunity, threat, information gap, and alternative intelligence reports without trade authority.

## EO-027 Multi-Office Intelligence Fusion Office

Fusion Office consumes existing Seeker reports and deterministically generates MIR, CFR, and ICR synthesis reports while preserving conflicts and source evidence.

## EO-028 Seeker Operational Readiness Review

Seeker readiness verifies all discovery offices, Fusion Office, report replay, audit reconstruction, independent evidence scores, and authorization to proceed to Group 4.
