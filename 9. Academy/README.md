# 9. Academy

User-facing deterministic, evidence-based financial education derived from validated ARGOS knowledge and doctrine.

## EO-078 Academy Framework

The Academy Framework is implemented as ARGOS's user-facing educational organization. It establishes the Academy mission, educational philosophy, organizational structure, educational pipeline, financial competency model, personalized learning framework, governance requirements, educational success metrics, deterministic educational principles, and complete governing prompt.

## EO-079 Instruction Office

The Instruction Office is implemented as the Academy's educational translation engine. It establishes instruction philosophy, lesson architecture, translation methodology, historical learning, decision reconstruction, personalized instruction, lesson validation, educational metrics, deterministic instructional principles, and the complete governing prompt.

## EO-080 Curriculum Office

The Curriculum Office is implemented as the Academy's educational architect. It designs competency-driven curriculum maps, prerequisite graphs, personalized learning pathways, curriculum version history, adaptation records, traceability records, curriculum effectiveness metrics, dashboards, and the complete governing prompt.

## EO-081 Knowledge Assessment Office

The Knowledge Assessment Office is implemented as the Academy's empirical measurement engine. It establishes competency assessment, decision-based evaluation, adaptive testing, student competency profiles, educational feedback, longitudinal learning analytics, assessment validation, metrics, dashboards, deterministic assessment principles, and the complete governing prompt.

## EO-082 Case Study Office

The Case Study Office is implemented as the Academy's experiential learning engine. It establishes case study architecture, historical reconstruction, decision simulation, multi-perspective analysis, alternative history scenarios, personalized case selection, validation, educational analytics, dashboards, deterministic case study principles, and the complete governing prompt.

## EO-083 Finance Tutor Office

The Finance Tutor Office is implemented as the Academy's conversational mentoring interface. It establishes personalized tutoring, adaptive guidance, Socratic reasoning, explanation depth, decision coaching, misconception detection, continuous coaching, educational conversation modeling, tutor analytics, deterministic tutoring principles, and the complete governing prompt.

## EO-084 Academy Fusion Office

The Academy Fusion Office is implemented as the Academy's educational intelligence center. It establishes educational integration, student educational modeling, personalized learning orchestration, cross-office coordination, educational optimization, learning feedback loops, quality assurance, Academy analytics, deterministic educational principles, and the complete governing prompt.

## EO-085 Academy Operational Readiness Review

The Academy Operational Readiness Review is implemented as the Academy's deterministic certification authority. It validates office certification, educational lifecycle, instruction traceability, curriculum integrity, assessment reliability, personalized learning, historical case authenticity, educational quality, stress testing, executive approval, corrective actions, and completion of ARGOS's initial deterministic cognitive enterprise architecture.
