# 4. Analyst Group

Reasoning, hypothesis generation, evidence evaluation, alternative explanations, and analytical assessment workflows.

## EO-029 Analyst Department Framework

Analyst Department consumes existing Seeker and Fusion reports, generates deterministic hypotheses, evaluates evidence objectively, records alternative explanations, and produces Analytical Assessment Reports without discovery or trade authority.

## EO-030 Statistical Analysis Office

Statistical Analysis Office produces deterministic quantitative AARs with probability estimates, confidence intervals, expected values, sensitivity analysis, model comparison, and mandatory argument maps.

## EO-031 Technical Analysis Office

Analyst-side Technical Analysis Office reasons over existing technical intelligence, generates Technical Argument Graphs, evaluates historical pattern reliability, and records alternative technical explanations.

## EO-032 Fundamental Analysis Office

Fundamental Analysis Office performs deterministic business analysis, generates Fundamental Reasoning Graphs, scores Business Quality, evaluates Economic Moat, and records alternative business explanations.

## EO-033 Macroeconomic Analysis Office

Macroeconomic Analysis Office performs deterministic macro reasoning, classifies economic regimes, generates Macroeconomic Reasoning Graphs, and preserves competing thesis analysis.

## EO-034 Derivatives Analysis Office

Derivatives Analysis Office performs deterministic derivatives reasoning, generates Derivatives Reasoning Graphs, preserves competing thesis analysis, and produces Probability Landscapes.

## EO-035 Behavioral Analysis Office

Behavioral Analysis Office performs deterministic behavioral reasoning, generates Decision Models, preserves competing thesis analysis, and produces Behavioral Probability Landscapes.

## EO-036 Risk Interaction Office

Risk Interaction Office pressure-tests Analyst Decision Models, generates Risk Readiness Reports, Analytical Stress Tests, Decision Model Challenge Reports, and confidence adjustment recommendations without overriding the Risk Office.

## EO-037 Cross-Discipline Review Office

Cross-Discipline Review Office performs deterministic peer review, generates Consensus Reports, Disagreement Reports, Analytical Conflict Reports, and preserves conflicting assumptions without forcing consensus.

## EO-038 Analytical Fusion Office

Analytical Fusion Office integrates Analyst work into a Unified Decision Model, Organizational Reasoning Graph, and Analytical Fusion Report while preserving provenance, disagreement, uncertainty, and source traceability.

## EO-039 Analyst Operational Readiness Review

Analyst Operational Readiness Review verifies all Analyst offices, validates analytical artifacts and Organizational Belief States, confirms replay and audit reconstruction, generates A-ORR and ACR-001, and authorizes Group 5 Risk Office.

Analysis workflows that transform collected information into structured evidence and decision-support material.
